Frequently Asked Questions

How do I compress an image to exactly 50KB?

Upload your image, enter 50 in the Target KB field, and click Compress. EditPDFForm automatically adjusts quality to hit the exact target.

Can I compress PNG to 50KB?

Yes. PNG, JPG and WEBP are all supported. For very small targets, converting PNG to JPG first often yields better results.

Is there a minimum achievable size?

For a typical photo, 10–20KB is the practical minimum while retaining recognisable quality. Going below that will produce a visibly blurry image.
