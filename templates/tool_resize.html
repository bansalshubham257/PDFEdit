Frequently Asked Questions

How do I resize to exactly 200×200?

Select Dimensions mode, enter 200 for both width and height, and turn off "Keep ratio" if your image isn't square. Click Resize Image.

Will resizing change the file format?

No. The output keeps the same format as your input (JPG stays JPG, PNG stays PNG etc.).

Can I resize multiple images at once?

Currently each image is resized individually. Batch resize is on our roadmap.
