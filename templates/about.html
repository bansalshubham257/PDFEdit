About Us

About EditPDFForm

The free, privacy-first online PDF editor and form filler — built for everyone. No Adobe. No sign-up. Ever.

What is EditPDFForm?

EditPDFForm is a free online PDF editor and form filler that runs entirely in your browser. Our primary focus is making it easy to fill PDF forms online, edit PDF files, sign PDFs and work with PDF documents — without Adobe Acrobat, without registration, and without paying anything.

We also offer a full suite of image tools — compress, resize, convert, crop, add effects and more — as addon tools for everyday file tasks.

Our Core PDF Tools

📋 Fill PDF Form Online

Our flagship tool. Fill any PDF form in your browser — type text in fields, tick checkboxes, complete government forms, job applications, bank forms and more. Works with both interactive and scanned PDFs.

✏️ Edit PDF Online

Add text anywhere on a PDF, insert images, draw shapes, highlight text and annotate. A full browser-based PDF editor without Adobe.

✍️ Sign PDF Online

Draw your handwritten signature, type your name in a signature font, or upload a signature image and place it on any PDF document.

Privacy First

All processing happens on our server during your session only. Uploaded files are never stored permanently — they are deleted immediately after you download your result. We do not track individual file contents, sell data, or require account creation.

Free Forever

EditPDFForm is and will remain free. We believe powerful PDF and file tools should be accessible to everyone — especially students, job seekers, and individuals filling government forms.
