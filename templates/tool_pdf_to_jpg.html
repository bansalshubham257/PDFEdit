How to Convert PDF to JPG

  1. Click Convert PDF to JPG → scroll to PDF Tools → Convert → PDF to Images
  2. Upload your PDF file
  3. Choose output format (JPG or PNG) and DPI (150 for screen, 300 for print)
  4. Optionally select specific pages
  5. Click Convert to Images — all pages download as a ZIP

Frequently Asked Questions

What DPI should I use for best quality?

Use 150 DPI for screen display and web use. Use 300 DPI for printing. Higher DPI produces larger file sizes.

Can I convert just one page of a PDF?

Yes. Enter a page number or range (e.g. "1" or "2-5") in the Pages field before converting.
