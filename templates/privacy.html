Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2025

1. Overview

EditPDFForm ("we", "us", "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

2. Files You Upload

Files uploaded to EditPDFForm are processed on our servers during your active session only. We do not store, retain, or access the contents of your files. Files are deleted from our temporary processing directory immediately after your download completes, or within 10 minutes of inactivity, whichever comes first.

We do not share, sell, or transmit your files to any third party.

3. Information We Collect Automatically

Like most websites, our server logs may temporarily record:

  • Your IP address (anonymised after 24 hours)
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring URL

This information is used solely for diagnosing technical issues and understanding aggregate usage patterns. It is never linked to the files you process.

4. Cookies

EditPDFForm uses only essential session cookies required for the application to function. We do not use advertising cookies, tracking pixels, or third-party analytics cookies.

5. Third-Party Services

We use Google Fonts for typography. Accessing our site causes your browser to request fonts from Google's servers. Please review Google's Privacy Policy for details.

6. Children's Privacy

EditPDFForm is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.

8. Contact

For privacy-related questions, contact us at our contact page or email privacy@editpdfform.com.
